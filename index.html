About AWGP Europe

Our Mission

All World Gayatri Pariwar (AWGP) is a socio-spiritual organization founded by Pt. Shriram Sharma Acharya. Our fundamental mission is the "Awakening of Divinity in Human Beings and the Descent of Heavenly Atmosphere on Earth."

AWGP Europe acts as a central hub for all devotees and seekers located across the European continent. We strive to spread the message of scientific spirituality, cultural upliftment, and holistic well-being through the timeless wisdom of the Gayatri Mantra and Yagya.

Join the Pariwar

We invite everyone, regardless of caste, creed, or nationality, to join this movement of self-transformation and world peace.

Our Core Activities

Discover the various initiatives and programs run by AWGP Europe to promote spiritual growth and community welfare.

Gayatri Yagya

Regular collective Yagyas performed for environmental purification and spiritual elevation. We organize both intimate home Yagyas and large-scale Ashwamedha Yagyas.

Sanskar Traditions

Providing sacramental rites (Sanskars) such as Vidyarambha, Punsavan, and Vivah absolutely free of cost to instill ethical values at every stage of life.

Bal Sanskar Shala

Weekend moral education classes for children across Europe. We teach Indian culture, meditation, and life management skills through engaging activities.

Divine Literature

Pt. Shriram Sharma Acharya wrote over 3,200 books on almost all aspects of human life. Explore our rich repository of spiritual literature.
